About

Deep Network GmbH is a Munich based software house and a consultancy company founded by ex-Microsoft developers.

Expertise

At Deep Network GmbH, our six decades of expertise in product engineering, the development stack, and cloud-based infrastructure is our greatest asset. Few consultancies bring the same technical depth and breadth to their relationships with large ISVs and software development teams.

Business first

Technology comes a distant second to the real issue: your business goals. By taking the time to clarify those goals, understand the way you operate, and explore the ways you define success, we can envision the best solution to get you there. Business first. Technology second.

Most profitable and sustainable direction

We envision the ways in which cloud technologies can take your company in the most profitable and sustainable direction. This requires not only a deep understanding of the technology itself, but an exploration of the far-ranging effects it has on every aspect of your business, from the development process to resource allocation to customer engagement and the sales and marketing cycle

Clear cost and performance projections

The breadth and depth of our expertise enables us to take a holistic approach that examines every facet of your business before proposing a solution. We identify key people across your organization who need to be part of the process. We run the numbers on different scenarios and offer clear cost and performance projections. We uncover hidden challenges and overlooked opportunities. And our focus is always firmly on your company’s long-term future.

Data/Big data

Rise of the software with its reach into every aspect of the business together with the advancements in networking meant one thing: more data. More data not only because we can have access to more data but now we can do more with data.


We can help you make the most out of your data:


-Data collection from your own or 3rd party apps and services.


-Data Visualisations and dashboards to present your data in a meaningful way via Azure and PowerBI.


-Data Visualisations on the web via frameworks like D3.js.


-Data processing pipelines on various platforms including Azure via ADLA/U-SQL, Databricks, Sql Warehouse to better analyze your data.


-Machine learning model training for Classification & Regression problems, deployment and management as a service. -DB Operations: Microsoft SQL Server, PostgreSQL (deploy, scale, maintain, design)
